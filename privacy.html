Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Sarthak URL, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • URLs: The original URLs you submit for shortening.
  • Custom Aliases: Any custom aliases you choose for your short links.
  • API Keys: Generated API keys are stored securely for authentication.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics (click counts, referrers) to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our URL shortening service.
  • To generate and manage your short links and custom aliases.
  • To improve our service and user experience.
  • To prevent abuse and ensure service security.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain your shortened URLs and associated data as long as they remain active or as needed for analytical purposes.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your shortened URLs and associated data.
  • You can revoke your API key at any time.
  • You can contact us to request a copy of your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Please review it periodically.
